
Question 1
What is the order of growth of the solution to the Quicksort-like recurrence 
Fn = 2N + 1 + 1/N ∑ 1<=k<=N (Fk-1 + FN-k) with F0 = 0/?

Answer:-
NlogN
                        OR 

What is the order of growth of the solution to the Quicksort-like recurrence
Fn = N^2 + 1 + 1/N ∑ 1<=k<=N (Fk-1 + FN-k) with F0 = 0?

Answer:-
N^2.

What is the order of growth of the solution to the Quicksort-like recurrence
FN = 2N + 1 + 1/N + ∑ 1≤K≤N (Fk - 1 + FN - K) with F0 = 0?

1. 0
2. 1
3. 1/N
4. N
5. N log N
6. $ N^2$
7. $ N^3$

Answer:- 5. N log N
